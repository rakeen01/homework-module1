Our Cliffs

Great Cliffs is home to four spectacular cliff regions, each offering unique views and experiences. Whether you enjoy a calm seaside stroll or an adventurous hike, our cliffs have something for everyone.

West Cliff

Famous for its dramatic sunsets and rugged coastline, West Cliff is a favorite among hikers and photographers alike.

North Cliff

Known for family-friendly trails and picnic spots, North Cliff is perfect for a relaxing day outdoors.

East Cliff

Offering challenging hikes and stunning panoramic views, East Cliff is a must for thrill-seekers and seasoned adventurers.

South Cliff

With calm waters and beginner-friendly activities, South Cliff is a great destination for kayaking and casual exploration.
